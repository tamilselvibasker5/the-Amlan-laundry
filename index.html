About The Amlan Laundry

Welcome to The Amlan Laundry. We are committed to strengthening local economies and empowering communities to achieve financial independence through organized laundry services. Our program is a transformative, low-investment laundry business and Self-Income Generating Program (SIGP) based in Kolkata, West Bengal, India.

We help traditional dhobi families looking to modernize, women and homemakers seeking financial independence, differently-abled individuals, unemployed youth starting their entrepreneurial journey, and aspiring small business owners to set up successful commercial laundry operations.

Our Services & Commercial Equipment

Our business package provides a complete business setup, including store design, branding name, high-performance LG commercial laundry equipment, comprehensive hands-on training, skilled staff placement support, and localized marketing.

  • LG Commercial Washers - Direct Drive technology, coin-operated system
  • LG 10kg Dryers - Compact yet powerful with advanced sensor technology
  • LG Titan 15kg Dryers - Maximum capacity electric dryer for high-volume
  • LG Gas Dryers - Cost-effective gas-powered solution

Comprehensive Training Modules

Our training modules cover commercial laundry equipment operations, advanced fabric care, stain removal, workflow management, safety protocols, and pricing strategies for profit optimization. We also partner with corporate CSR programs, banks, and government self-employment schemes to support candidates with financing and setups.
